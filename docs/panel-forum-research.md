## Recommended upgrades (5 fields each)

### POWER

**1. Lithium (LiFePO4) battery conversion**
- **Type:** Both (factory option on many 2024+ trims e.g. Pottery Barn/Classic come w/ Battle Born; aftermarket conversion extremely common on older rigs).
- **Why:** Owners say you get the *full* rated amp-hours (vs ~50% usable on lead-acid), roughly "quadruple your usable energy," far lighter/compact, drop-in to the stock battery box (Epoch 300Ah noted as fitting). Enables real boondocking.
- **Consensus:** VERY STRONG. Named by multiple owners in the top-3-mods thread; an entire dedicated multi-owner thread; the Outside owner did it first. Repeated caveat: you must also swap the **converter/charger to a lithium charging profile** and add a **DC-DC charger** and/or **shunt/monitor** — owners treat these as a package, not separate optional items.
- **Source (verified):** airforums #1443198 ✓; airforums #1448404 ✓; Outside Online ✓

**2. Rooftop and/or portable solar**
- **Type:** Both (factory "solar prewire" common; owners add panels).
- **Why:** Recharge off-grid without a generator. Active debate owners genuinely have: **rooftop** (charges while driving, nothing to set up/store) vs **portable/free-standing** (park in shade, chase the sun, but "no place to store them" is the recurring complaint). Many run both.
- **Consensus:** VERY STRONG, but note the rooftop-vs-portable tradeoff is a real ongoing debate, not settled. 600W roof setups mentioned repeatedly.
- **Source (verified):** airforums #1443198 (multiple owners: 600W, 510W roof) ✓; airforums #1448404 (long portable-vs-roof discussion) ✓; Outside Online ✓

**3. Inverter / inverter-charger (hybrid)**
- **Type:** Both.
- **Why:** Run AC outlets / small appliances off battery; "go to a hybrid inverter/charger" listed as a core optional add. Victron MultiPlus/Quattro named repeatedly.
- **Consensus:** MODERATE-STRONG. Listed by owners but more often by the heavier-build crowd; the Outside owner deliberately *skipped* an inverter (used a portable power station instead), so it's not universal for casual campers.
- **Source (verified):** airforums #1443198 ("hybrid inverter/charger") ✓; airforums #1448404 (Victron Quattro/MultiPlus) ✓; Outside Online (chose NOT to) ✓

**4. DC-DC charger**
- **Type:** Aftermarket (part of lithium conversion).
- **Why:** Modern tow vehicles have "smart" alternators; a DC-DC charger (Victron Orion XS named) lets you charge lithium from the truck while driving without blowing the 7-pin fuse. Owners call it "best practice."
- **Consensus:** STRONG *within the lithium-conversion context* — it's almost always discussed as a required companion to lithium, not a standalone headline mod.
- **Source (verified):** airforums #1448404 ✓

### CLIMATE

**5. A/C soft start (Micro-Air EasyStart / SoftStartRV)**
- **Type:** Aftermarket.
- **Why:** Lets the A/C start on a smaller generator and "can run everything all at once," plus quiets the "abrupt start-up noises." A genuine repeat mention.
- **Consensus:** MODERATE-STRONG. Appears in owner top-mod lists and the community-poll YouTube video; well-known by name.
- **Source (verified):** airforums #1443198 ("Soft Starts — can run everything all at once with generator"; another owner "MicroAire easy start") ✓

**6. Replace Fantastic vent fan with MaxxAir (Maxxfan)**
- **Type:** Aftermarket.
- **Why:** Better airflow/ventilation; owners specifically "replace Magic Fan with Maxaair" / "MaxxFans replacement of Fantastics." Some add MaxxShade/pillow covers.
- **Consensus:** STRONG among the top-mods thread (named by at least 3 separate owners). **This is currently MISSING from the page's 19 and the community clearly loves it.**
- **Source (verified):** airforums #1443198 ✓

### TOWING / SAFETY

**7. Weight-distribution + anti-sway hitch**
- **Type:** Aftermarket (dealer-installed at delivery).
- **Why:** Safe towing of a heavy trailer; controls sway. The club frames the brake controller + proper hitch as non-negotiable.
- **Consensus:** STRONG as foundational safety, though owners discuss it less as an "upgrade" and more as a given. The club list emphasizes brake controller as legally required.
- **Source (verified):** Airstream Club "36 Things" (#1 Brake Controller, hitch framing) ✓

**8. TPMS (tire pressure monitoring, TST brand)**
- **Type:** Aftermarket.
- **Why:** "You can't feel low tire pressure or a flat while towing" — critical safety; the club explicitly recommends the **TST** brand and its repeater.
- **Consensus:** STRONG. In the club's official Highly Recommended tier by name; LevelMate/leveling come up alongside in owner lists.
- **Source (verified):** Airstream Club "36 Things" #8 ✓

**9. Surge protector / EMS (Progressive Industries)**
- **Type:** Aftermarket.
- **Why:** "Better than a surge protector" — protects A/C, wiring, electronics from campground brownouts/surges/faulty wiring. Club recommends **Progressive** by name; 50A if two A/Cs.
- **Consensus:** STRONG. In club Highly Recommended tier; owners mention hardwired Progressive 50A EMS.
- **Source (verified):** Airstream Club "36 Things" #9 ✓; airforums #1443198 ("Progressive hardwired 50A EMS") ✓

**10. Leveling system — blocks/chocks + LevelMate Pro**
- **Type:** Aftermarket.
- **Why:** "Makes leveling so easy" — repeatedly cited as a favorite. X-chocks + Andersen levelers + the **LevelMate Pro** Bluetooth app come up again and again; one owner called LevelMate "probably my most favorite improvement."
- **Consensus:** VERY STRONG. Among the most-named items in the owner top-mods thread; chocks/blocks are in the club Essential tier. The **LevelMate Pro specifically** is a community darling and is arguably MISSING as a named item from the current 19 (page lists blocks/chocks but not the app/sensor).
- **Source (verified):** airforums #1443198 (named by 3+ owners) ✓; Airstream Club "36 Things" #4 (blocks), #21 (chocks) ✓

### WATER

**11. Inline / multi-stage water filter**
- **Type:** Aftermarket.
- **Why:** Campground water quality varies; owners filter incoming water (Blu Tech 3-stage MR3 named) plus a drinking filter inside (Berkey/Seagull/Acuva). "Best water system we've had."
- **Consensus:** STRONG. In club Highly Recommended tier (Blu Tech) AND multiple owner lists (Blu Tech MR3, Seagull IV, Acuva).
- **Source (verified):** Airstream Club "36 Things" #16 ✓; airforums #1443198 ✓

**12. Water pressure regulator**
- **Type:** Aftermarket.
- **Why:** Protect plumbing from high campground pressure. Standard accepted gear (less *discussed* because it's cheap/obvious).
- **Consensus:** MODERATE. Universally owned but rarely debated; I did NOT see it called out as a "favorite mod" in the threads I read — it's assumed baseline. Honest signal: foundational, not a passion item.
- **Source (verified):** Implied across club gear framing; no strong standalone forum endorsement captured. (Weakest of the water items — flag.)

**13. Shower upgrade — recirc/mixing valve or better shower head (Oxygenics)**
- **Type:** Aftermarket.
- **Why:** "Best mod — Oxygenic shower head. Great shower pressure and uses hardly any water." Others add a shower head with full cutoff valve. Saves water for boondocking.
- **Consensus:** MODERATE-STRONG. The shower-head/cutoff version is owner-loved; the recirc/mixing-valve version shows up mostly in creator videos (secondary).
- **Source (verified):** airforums #1443198 (Oxygenic head; "shower head with separate complete cutoff") ✓

### INTERIOR

**14. Window film/tint + clear (tinted) door glass**
- **Type:** Aftermarket.
- **Why:** Heat/UV reduction; "Stop the heat." Owners also swap the door's screen-guard glass for **clear glass** for better visibility.
- **Consensus:** MODERATE. Tint appears in owner lists and creator content; clear door glass is a popular small mod ("clear glass in door").
- **Source (verified):** airforums #1443198 ("Clear glass in door"; "Tinted front windows") ✓

**15. Glass skylight (Maxim)**
- **Type:** Aftermarket.
- **Why:** Replaces the plastic factory skylight/vent with glass for light + better look. Named brand "Maxim" recurs.
- **Consensus:** MODERATE-STRONG. "Maxim skylights" named by at least 2 owners in the top-mods thread.
- **Source (verified):** airforums #1443198 (two owners list "Maxim/Maxim skylights") ✓

**16. 170° soft-close cabinet hinges**
- **Type:** Aftermarket.
- **Why:** Full closet/cabinet access; fixes restricted factory hinges. Common DIY fix-it mod.
- **Consensus:** MODERATE. Strong in creator "fixes" content (the "7 Fixes" video) and a known pain point; I did NOT capture it as a top-mod in the owner threads, so consensus is "fix-it popular" rather than "passion upgrade."
- **Source (verified):** Appears in YouTube fixes content (secondary). Owner-thread signal: weak. Honest flag.

**17. Anti-theft hitch/coupler locks**
- **Type:** Both (Airstream sells an Anti-Theft Lock Kit; aftermarket common).
- **Why:** "Lock your whole home" on the road; tongue/coupler lock deters drive-away theft.
- **Consensus:** MODERATE. In the Airstream beginner gear framing and owner lists ("Chrome locks," "Locks on overhead sliding doors"); steady but not a hot topic.
- **Source (verified):** airforums #1443198 ("Chrome locks") ✓; Airstream Club/beginner gear framing ✓

## Missing mods the community genuinely loves

Ranked roughly by strength of verified signal:

1. **LED lighting conversion** — owners explicitly "converted all the lights to LED," "warm white LED bulb replacements." Cheap, repeated, universally liked. The Outside owner did it; the club notes older rigs need it. **Strong, currently missing as a named item.**
2. **MaxxAir / Maxxfan vent fan upgrade (replace Fantastic/Magic Fan)** — named by 3+ owners. **Strong, missing.**
3. **LevelMate Pro (Bluetooth leveling sensor/app)** — community darling; "my most favorite improvement." Page lists blocks/chocks but not this. **Strong, missing as named item.**
4. **3-inch lift kit (Dexter Torflex spacer) + 16" wheel/Michelin tire upgrade** — repeatedly the #1 listed mod by multiple owners ("Lift kit" tops several lists); enables larger tires + clearance. Note: more relevant to off-road/older rigs and changes towing geometry. **Very strong in forums, missing.** (Scope caveat: it's a structural mod, present honestly.)
5. **Mattress upgrade (Airstream Supply Co / Douglas / latex topper)** — extremely common complaint that factory mattresses are too hard; owners swap or top them. **Strong, missing.**
6. **Composting toilet (OGO, etc.) + black-tank delete** — the Outside owner's single favorite upgrade; saves water, adds clearance/cargo. Polarizing but passionately loved by boondockers. **Moderate-strong (one deep source + known community split), missing.**
7. **Brake controller** — club lists it FIRST and calls it legally required. If the page covers towing/safety it's a glaring omission. **Strong, missing.**
8. **Starlink / cellular (5G) internet + booster** — heavy presence in installer builds (Peplink 5G, 7-in-1 antenna, Starlink integration). Signal is mostly secondary (installer/YouTube), so present as "trending" not "forum-proven." **Moderate, missing.**
9. **Replace factory converter with lithium-profile converter (Progressive Dynamics / WFCO lithium)** — required companion to lithium; owners stress it constantly. Could be folded into the lithium item. **Strong (within lithium), partially missing.**
10. **Battery monitor / shunt + battery cutoff switch (Victron SmartShunt, Blue Sea)** — repeatedly cited as part of any lithium/solar build. **Strong (within power), missing.**
11. **GasStop propane emergency shut-off + Mopeka tank sensors** — club Highly Recommended (GasStop); Mopeka in owner lists. **Moderate, missing.**
12. **Backup/rear-view camera upgrade (HaloView)** — multiple owners; helps backing in. **Moderate, missing.**
13. **Induction cooktop / portable induction burner** — owners cook without propane fumes; pairs with lithium. **Moderate, missing.**
14. **On-demand (tankless) water heater** — mostly vendor/creator signal (GT Services). **Weak-moderate, missing — present cautiously.**
15. **Insulated/magnetic window covers (4-season camping)** — owner cold-weather builds (Strawfoot custom covers). **Moderate, missing.**
16. **Dehumidifier / Hypervent under-mattress pad (anti-mold)** — one owner detailed Hypervent pad to fight condensation against aluminum walls. **Weak-moderate, missing.**
17. **Disc brake conversion + axle replacement** — appears in owner top-lists for older/heavier rigs. **Niche but real, missing.**
