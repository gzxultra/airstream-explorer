## Detailed Findings

### ✅ Solid — present as-is
- **Battle Born 100Ah**: $799 sale (reg $949), 10-yr warranty. Confirmed at battlebornbatteries.com.
- **Epoch Elite V2T**: 460Ah, built-in 400A EATON Class-T fuse, internal heater, Victron comms, 11-yr warranty. ~$2,199 sale / $2,399 MSRP. Confirmed at epochbatteries.com.
- **Airstream factory lithium**: Trade Wind = 810Ah heated (3×270Ah Battle Born) + 600W solar. Confirmed at airstream.com.
- **Victron MultiPlus-II 12/3000/120-50 2×120V (UL)**: ~$1,088 street; claimed range $1,088–1,375 is reasonable.
- **Micro-Air EasyStart 364**: ~$313; 65–75% start-current reduction confirmed (Citimarine). Claim "~70% (to ~25–30A from 60–100A)" defensible.
- **2nd A/C w/ heat pump**: $2,200; boosts to 50A; offered on listed Intl/Globetrotter floorplans, excluded on 30RB. Confirmed at support.airstream.com.
- **TST 507 TPMS**: 6-sensor flow-through kit $446.02 — exact match to claim.
- **Progressive EMS-HW50C**: $187–380 street. Claim "~$100–350+" works as a category range if framed as surge-strip → full-EMS.
- **Andersen / X-chocks**: "~$40–150" is a reasonable category range.

### ⚠️ Needs a number fix
1. **AirSkirts** — Claimed **$1,000–1,600**, actual **$1,958–$2,768** for Airstream-specific kits (airskirts.com). This is significantly too low; update.
2. **Equal-i-zer** — Claimed **$500–700**, actual **$750–950** ($750 without shank, $925+ for the 16K model with shank).
3. **ProPride 3P** — Claimed **$2,995–3,645**; low end too low. Use **~$3,300–3,695** (ProPride store $3,645; Hensley comparison $3,695; typical $3,300–3,500). Jim Hensley pivot-design attribution is correct.
4. **Budget batteries (Power Queen / LiTime 100Ah)** — Claimed **$230–280**; actual ~**$170–230**. High end inflated, low end too high.

### ⚠️ Partially unverifiable — soften
- **Factory solar (Claim 2)**: Core facts solid (Trade Wind 600W std; Classic 300W std; 300W optional ~$2,600 on Globetrotter/International/Flying Cloud). But **Basecamp 360W-with-lithium tier** and **"factory lithium only sold bundled with solar"** could not be sourced — soften or drop.
- **Water/shower (Claim 11)**: Camco inline filter (~$15–21) and pressure regulator ($15–60) are fine. **Shower recirculation/mixing valve "$30–120"** was not sourceable — soften or drop.

### ❌ Unverifiable — recommend dropping specific numbers
- **Claim 12 (window tint / Maxim glass skylight / 170° hinges)**: No primary sourcing obtained. These are generic aftermarket items with very wide price ranges; do not present specific dollar figures as verified.
- **SoftStartRV** (mentioned under Claim 4): specific pricing not separately verified.
